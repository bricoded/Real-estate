Property Description

About this home Welcome to 4205 Ohio St UNIT 207, a stunning residence nestled in the heart of San Diego's vibrant and trendy North Park neighborhood. Upgraded with new energy efficient appliances and new flooring less than 2 years old! This modern and renovated stylish condominium offers an exceptional living experience, combining contemporary design with a convenient location. As you step inside, you'll be greeted by an inviting and light-filled open floor plan. The living area boasts a seamless flow, providing ample space for relaxation and entertaining with a new upgraded kitchen. Large windows bathe the space in natural light, creating a warm and welcoming ambiance.

The unit comprises two spacious bedrooms, offering comfortable and private retreats. The primary bedroom boasts generous closet space and an en-suite bathroom, complete with modern fixtures and finishes. Washer and dryer are smart capable and controllable from mobile device. The second bedroom is versatile and can be utilized as a guest room, home office, or additional living space to suit your preferences. Situated in the heart of North Park, this residence offers an unparalleled lifestyle. Within walking distance, you'll find an eclectic array of dining options, trendy boutiques, craft breweries, and entertainment venues.
